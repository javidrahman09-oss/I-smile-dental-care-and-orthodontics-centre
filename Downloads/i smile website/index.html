Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit — answered clearly and honestly.

Is the treatment painful? +
Most of our treatments are completely pain-free thanks to modern anaesthesia and our gentle techniques. We take extra care to ensure your comfort throughout every procedure.
How long do braces take? +
Treatment duration varies by case — typically 12 to 24 months for braces. During your consultation we'll give you a precise timeline based on your specific needs.
Do you treat children? +
Absolutely! We have a dedicated paediatric dental approach designed to make children comfortable and confident. We recommend first dental visits as early as age 2-3.
What is the cost of teeth whitening? +
Costs vary based on the whitening method and severity of discolouration. We offer affordable options for all budgets. Book a consultation for a precise quote.
How often should I visit the dentist? +
We recommend a check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months. Regular visits allow us to catch issues early and keep your smile in perfect health.
Do you offer EMI or payment plans? +
Yes! We work with patients to make quality dental care affordable. Flexible payment options are available for larger treatments. Ask our team for details.
